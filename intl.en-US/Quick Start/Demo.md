# Demo

ApsaraVideo VOD provides a demo for you to experience the cloud-device integration solution. The demo demonstrates best practices of ApsaraVideo that connects devices by using the cloud. The demo covers a full range of ApsaraVideo services, including the short video SDK, player SDK, and upload SDK. The demo provides complete product-level user interface \(UI\) and business source code to help developers meet their business requirements at a lower cost.

![](https://static-aliyun-doc.oss-accelerate.aliyuncs.com/assets/img/en-US/1372815161/p185937.png)

You can scan the following QR code to download and install the demo.

![](https://static-aliyun-doc.oss-accelerate.aliyuncs.com/assets/img/en-US/2372815161/p185938.png)

**Note:** Supported systems: iOS 9.0 and later, and Android 4.3 and later.
